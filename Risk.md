## 1Ô∏è‚É£ **Plan Risk Management**

**Goal:**
Define how to approach and perform risk management.

**Key Inputs:**

* Project Charter
* Project Management Plan
* Stakeholder Register

**Tools & Techniques:**

* Expert Judgment
* Meetings
* Analytical Techniques

**Outputs:**

* **Risk Management Plan**

### üìò The Risk Management Plan includes:

* Methodology & tools
* Roles & responsibilities
* Funding and timing
* Risk categories (RBS ‚Äì Risk Breakdown Structure)
* Definitions of probability and impact
* Probability & impact matrix
* Risk thresholds (tolerance limits)

---

## 2Ô∏è‚É£ **Identify Risks**

**Goal:**
Document potential risks (threats + opportunities).

**Tools & Techniques:**

* **Brainstorming** (with team & experts)
* **Delphi technique** (anonymous expert consensus)
* **Root Cause Analysis**
* **SWOT Analysis (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats)**
* **Checklist analysis**
* **Prompt lists** (PESTLE, TECOP, VUCA models)
* **Assumption & constraint analysis**

**Outputs:**

* **Risk Register**

  * List of identified risks
  * Potential causes & effects
  * Risk owner (person responsible)
  * Initial responses
* **Risk Report**

  * Summary of overall risk exposure

üß† *Tip:* The Risk Register evolves throughout the project ‚Äî it‚Äôs a **living document**.

---

## 3Ô∏è‚É£ **Perform Qualitative Risk Analysis**

**Goal:**
Prioritize risks for further action based on *probability* and *impact*.

**Tools & Techniques:**

* Probability and Impact Matrix
* Risk Categorization
* Risk Urgency Assessment
* Expert Judgment
* Meetings

**Outputs:**

* Updated **Risk Register** (with ranking or scoring)
* Updated **Risk Report**

‚úÖ *Key outcome:* High-priority risks are selected for deeper (quantitative) analysis.

---

## 4Ô∏è‚É£ **Perform Quantitative Risk Analysis**

**Goal:**
Numerically analyze the *combined effect* of identified risks on project objectives.

**Tools & Techniques:**

* **Monte Carlo Simulation** (probabilistic modeling)
* **Sensitivity Analysis (Tornado Diagram)**
* **Expected Monetary Value (EMV)**
  [
  EMV = Probability \times Impact
  ]
* **Decision Tree Analysis**
* **Influence Diagrams**

**Outputs:**

* Quantified risk exposure
* Updated risk reports
* Inputs for contingency reserves

üìä *Example:*
If a risk has a 40% chance of causing ‚Çπ10,000 loss ‚Üí EMV = ‚Çπ4,000 (Expected loss).

## 5Ô∏è‚É£ **Plan Risk Responses**

**Goal:**
Develop options and actions to enhance opportunities and reduce threats.

---

### **Risk Response Strategies**

| Type                               | For | Strategies   | Description                                                               |
| ---------------------------------- | --- | ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Threats (Negative Risks)**       |     | **Avoid**    | Eliminate the threat (e.g., change scope or plan).                        |
|                                    |     | **Transfer** | Shift impact to a third party (e.g., insurance, warranty).                |
|                                    |     | **Mitigate** | Reduce probability or impact (e.g., testing, training).                   |
|                                    |     | **Accept**   | Take no action unless it occurs (passive) or plan a contingency (active). |
| **Opportunities (Positive Risks)** |     | **Exploit**  | Ensure opportunity occurs (assign best resources).                        |
|                                    |     | **Share**    | Partner with third party to realize benefit.                              |
|                                    |     | **Enhance**  | Increase probability/impact (e.g., improve conditions).                   |
|                                    |     | **Accept**   | Willing to take advantage if it occurs.                                   |

---

### **Outputs:**

* **Risk Response Plan** (integrated into project plan)
* Updates to Risk Register and Risk Report

---

## 6Ô∏è‚É£ **Implement Risk Responses**

**Goal:**

**Tools & Techniques:**

* Interpersonal skills (communication, influencing)
* Audits

**Outputs:**

* Change requests (if new actions required)
* Work performance information

---

## 7Ô∏è‚É£ **Monitor Risks**

**Goal:**

**Tools & Techniques:**

* Risk audits
* Risk reassessment
* Variance & trend analysis
* Meetings

**Outputs:**

* Work Performance Information
* Change Requests
* Updates to Risk Register & Risk Reports
